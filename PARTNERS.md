# Partner with Zenovay

Zenovay works with boilerplate, starter-kit, template, and SaaS-kit maintainers
in three practical ways. Any public relationship is described accurately and
only after both sides agree.

## Community integration

- Zenovay implements and maintains the integration.
- The boilerplate maintainer reviews it.
- Zenovay supports users who activate it.

This is a good fit when a kit accepts community-maintained providers or keeps a
public integrations directory.

## Recommended provider

- Zenovay appears as an official analytics option.
- Boilerplate users may receive a relevant, mutually agreed benefit.
- Zenovay maintains documentation, examples, and user support.

No benefit, discount, or commercial term is implied until it is announced by
both parties.

## Built-in partnership

- Zenovay is preconfigured or offered during setup.
- An affiliate relationship may be added by agreement.
- A joint integration announcement is optional.
- Zenovay maintains the integration and supports users.

To explore a partnership, email `partners@zenovay.com` or open an integration
request in this repository. Please do not include credentials or private source
in an issue.
