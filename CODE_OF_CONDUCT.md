# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation welcoming and harassment-free for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing technical facts directly.
- Respect licenses, privacy, and maintainer boundaries.
- Keep sales outreach and unrelated promotion out of issues and pull requests.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, publishing private information,
or sustained disruption are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `community@zenovay.com`. Maintainers may
edit, remove, reject, or ban contributions or participants when necessary to
protect the community.
